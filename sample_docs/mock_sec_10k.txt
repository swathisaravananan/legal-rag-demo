PART I

ITEM 1. BUSINESS

Overview

TechVault Systems, Inc. is a leading provider of enterprise cloud security and data governance solutions. Founded in 2014, we serve over 2,400 customers worldwide including 37 of the Fortune 100 companies. Our platform enables organizations to discover, classify, protect, and govern sensitive data across hybrid and multi-cloud environments.

Our primary products include:

TechVault DataShield — Our flagship data security posture management (DSPM) platform. DataShield uses machine learning to identify PII, PHI, and financial data with 99.2% accuracy.

TechVault PolicyEngine — A policy orchestration layer managing over 4.2 billion policy decisions per day across our customer base.

TechVault AuditTrail — An immutable, blockchain-anchored audit logging service certified for SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA, and FedRAMP Moderate compliance.

The data security market is projected to reach $38.4 billion by 2027 at a CAGR of 22.3%.

ITEM 1A. RISK FACTORS

We have incurred net losses of $48.3 million, $61.7 million, and $79.2 million for fiscal years 2021, 2022, and 2023 respectively. As of December 31, 2023, we had an accumulated deficit of $267.4 million. We may not achieve profitability in the foreseeable future.

We face intense competition from Palo Alto Networks, Microsoft, IBM Security, and specialized data governance providers.

ITEM 7. MANAGEMENT'S DISCUSSION AND ANALYSIS

Total revenue increased 35% year-over-year to $267.3 million in fiscal 2023. Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) reached $301.4 million as of December 31, 2023, up from $214.7 million at end of fiscal 2022, representing 40% growth. Net revenue retention rate was 118%.

Subscription revenue was $241.8 million (90% of total revenue), up 38% from $175.3 million in fiscal 2022.

Professional services revenue was $25.5 million (10% of total revenue).

Gross profit was $208.9 million, representing a gross margin of 78.2%, compared to 77.7% in fiscal 2022.

Research and development expenses were $98.3 million (36.8% of revenue).

Sales and marketing expenses were $141.7 million (53.0% of revenue).
